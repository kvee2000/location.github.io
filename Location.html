    Welcome to Atlanta

    Atlanta, Georgia, is a major cultural and economic hub in the southeastern United States, recognized for its vibrant arts scene, diverse culinary offerings, and as a transportation center with one of the busiest airports in the world. It serves as a key player in sectors such as finance, entertainment, and technology.
